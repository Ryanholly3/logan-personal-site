Project Goal

Design a professional, yet approachable platform for developers to build, search, and sell components. It was important to keep the cross-functional team in mind.

Research & Synthesis

When joining this we received research from the UX team, including: personas, competitive analysis, and low-fidelity wireframes. From here, we conducted our own research that included a visual competitive analysis along with speaking to our client directly to understand their vision for Digiline prior to beginning the first designs.

Competitive analysis synthesized with examples*

Design Principles

After initial research and data collection, we built out a set of design principles to keep in mind when building Digiline:

- Professional, yet approachable
- Modern design for the modern developer
- Clear defined work space vs substance
- Function over style
- Personalization and customization features for the users

Design Solutions: Mood Boards

We then took all research, client-feedback, and design principles to create divergent mood boards to begin our design ideas and get a more solidified direction from our client. This was our first attempt at taking the information and bringing it to life. It was important for us to find elements that our client invisioned, but also followed our research.
