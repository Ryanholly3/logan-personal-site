I am a Denver-based designer with 3 years of design experience following a career in recruitment. I switch to a career in design to satisfy my desire to build and create, and have found my passion in it. While working with clients, my experience focused on high fidelity prototyping, microinteractions, and responsive designs for mobile, web, and marketing sites.

One of my favorite things about working in design is the creative process of drawing inspiration from my surroundings, and using them to synthesize something new and exciting. Whether it be a color palette or feeling - I take these elements and combine them to build an entirely new experience for users.

While I am comfortable working on a wide range of projects, each company that I have worked for has had cultures that focus on collaboration, inclusivity, education, and sustainability. These values are important to me, and I hope to find these qualities in any role I step into.
