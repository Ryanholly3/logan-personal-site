About Me

I am a Denver-based designer with 6 years of professional experience in recruitment and human resources. I decided to switch to a career in design to satisfy my desire to build and create, and have found my passion in it. While working with clients, my experience focused on high fidelity prototyping, microinteractions, and responsive designs for mobile, web, and marketing sites.

In recruiting, I developed a strength in taking a loosely defined company goal or vision, and creating a well defined solution. I have taken this experience from recruitment and applied it to design - taking in a client’s abstract vision, and translating it into a well-defined solution. While working for clients, I excel at taking a vision and translating it into a stunning design.

One of my favorite things about working in design is the creative process of drawing inspiration from my surroundings, and using them to synthesize something new and exciting. Whether it be a grid, color palette, or feeling - I take these elements and combine them to build an entirely new experience for users.

While I am comfortable working on a wide range of projects, each company that I have worked for has had cultures that focus on collaboration, inclusivity, education, and sustainability. These values are important to me, and I hope to find these qualities in any role I step into.

Outside of my professional life, I enjoy cooking, camping, exercising, and spending time with my husband and puppy. I enjoy spending time in Colorado’s great outdoors, and often draw design inspiration from these landscapes. I also tap into my creativity while cooking. A common expression in cooking is that happy people make happy food - and I believe the same is true in design, happy people create good designs.
